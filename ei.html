Environmental Impact

Using solar energy has a positive impact on the environment by reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Our dashboard provides insights into the environmental benefits of using solar panels.

Key Benefits

  • Carbon Footprint Reduction: Estimate the reduction in CO2 emissions based on your solar energy generation.
  • Environmental Savings: See how your use of solar energy contributes to a cleaner environment.
  • Comparison with Fossil Fuels: Compare the environmental impact of solar energy versus traditional fossil fuel-based electricity.
  • Sustainability Metrics: Track sustainability metrics such as energy savings and CO2 avoided.
