Cost Savings

Utilizing solar energy can significantly reduce your electricity bills. Our dashboard helps you track your savings over time and provides insights into how much you are saving each month.

How It Works

  • Real-Time Monitoring: Track your energy production and usage in real-time to optimize your energy consumption.
  • Historical Analysis: Review historical data to understand your energy consumption patterns and savings over time.
  • Cost Calculations: Automatically calculate your savings based on your energy production and local electricity rates.
  • Energy Efficiency Tips: Receive personalized tips on how to improve your energy efficiency and maximize savings.
  • Environmental Impact: See how much CO2 emissions you've avoided by using solar energy.
